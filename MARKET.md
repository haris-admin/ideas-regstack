## Market Context

### The Regulatory Tsunami

Australian SMEs are being hit by **multiple regulatory waves simultaneously**:

```
Timeline:
Apr-May 2026: ASIC "Minute to Midnight" letter — enforcement acceleration
Jun 2026:     ASIC Digital Assets reporting requirements
1 Jul 2026:   AUSTRAC Tranche 2 — real estate agents, lawyers, accountants
31 Jul 2026:  EU AI Act effective (impacts AU exporters)
Aug 2026:     BNPL licensing consultation ends
TBD 2026:     AU AI Mandatory Guardrails legislation
TBD 2026:     Privacy Act reforms
TBD 2027:     NPP PayTo mandatory compliance
```

### The Incumbent

> **60%+ of AU SMEs still manage compliance using spreadsheets, Word documents, and email.**

This means:
- No centralised deadline tracking
- Manual form filing (errors, missed deadlines)
- No audit trail
- No regulatory change monitoring
- Heavy reliance on expensive external advisors

### Beachhead Validation

The AUSTRAC Tranche 2 deadline (1 July 2026) creates **buyer urgency**. Real estate agents who miss compliance face:
- AUSTRAC investigation
- Civil penalties up to $2.2M for individuals, $22M for bodies corporate
- Potential loss of registration/licence

This is a **comply-now** moment, not a nice-to-have.
